# Artificial Intelligence (AI-2002)

## Overview
This repository is dedicated to the **Artificial Intelligence (AI-2002)** course. It will be used throughout the semester to organize and maintain coursework related to the theoretical and practical understanding of artificial intelligence concepts.

The repository is initialized at the beginning of the semester and will be updated progressively as course content, assignments, and projects are introduced.

## Purpose
The primary objectives of this repository are to:
- Maintain a structured record of AI coursework over time  
- Track individual progress and development throughout the semester  
- Organize assignments and project-related material in a single location  
- Follow best practices for version control and documentation  

## Repository Structure
At this stage, the repository serves as a base setup. Relevant folders and files will be added gradually as the semester progresses.
